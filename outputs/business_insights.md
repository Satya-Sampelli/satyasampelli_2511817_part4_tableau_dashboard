# Business Insights

## 1. Sales Trend

**Observation:**
Sales remain fairly consistent throughout the two-year period, with Technology contributing the highest sales across most months.

**Business Interpretation:**
Technology is the strongest performing category and contributes a large share of overall revenue. This makes it an important driver of business performance.

**Recommended Action:**
Continue investing in high-performing Technology products while exploring opportunities to increase sales in the other categories.

---

## 2. Regional Performance

**Observation:**
The South region performs better than the other regions in both sales and profit.

**Business Interpretation:**
This suggests that the South region is operating more effectively or has stronger customer demand than the other regions.

**Recommended Action:**
Analyse the strategies used in the South region and determine whether similar approaches can be implemented in lower-performing regions.

---

## 3. Category Profitability

**Observation:**
Technology generates the highest profit, while some categories contribute much less despite generating sales.

**Business Interpretation:**
Not every category contributes equally to business profitability. High sales do not always result in high profits.

**Recommended Action:**
Focus on expanding high-profit products while reviewing the pricing and performance of lower-profit categories.

---

## 4. Customer Segment Performance

**Observation:**
Customer segments contribute relatively similar levels of sales, with Home Office performing slightly better overall.

**Business Interpretation:**
The business is not dependent on a single customer segment, which reduces overall business risk.

**Recommended Action:**
Maintain balanced marketing efforts while identifying opportunities to improve performance in the lower-performing customer segments.

---

## 5. Discount Impact

**Observation:**
The dashboard shows a negative relationship between discount levels and profit.

**Business Interpretation:**
Offering larger discounts may increase sales volume, but it can also reduce overall profitability.

**Recommended Action:**
Review discount policies and ensure promotions are increasing both sales and profit rather than simply increasing sales volume.

---

## 6. Shipping Performance

**Observation:**
Delivery performance varies across shipping modes, with Standard Class taking the longest delivery time.

**Business Interpretation:**
Longer delivery times may affect customer satisfaction and could contribute to product returns.

**Recommended Action:**
Monitor shipping performance regularly and look for ways to improve delivery efficiency where possible.

---

## 7. Return Analysis

**Observation:**
Return rates differ across product categories, with Furniture showing higher return levels than other categories.

**Business Interpretation:**
Higher return rates increase costs and reduce overall profitability.

**Recommended Action:**
Investigate the reasons behind higher Furniture returns and identify opportunities to improve product quality, packaging or customer expectations.

---

## 8. Overall Business Opportunity

**Observation:**
The dashboard combines sales, profit, customer, regional and product information into a single interactive view.

**Business Interpretation:**
Management can quickly identify both strong-performing areas and locations where additional attention is needed.

**Recommended Action:**
Use the dashboard regularly to monitor business performance, identify emerging trends and support data-driven decision making across the organisation.

---

# Summary

The Tableau dashboard provides a clear overview of the company's performance across multiple business areas. Technology and the South region are currently the strongest performers, while discounting, shipping performance and product returns present opportunities for further improvement. The interactive dashboard allows users to filter and explore the data, making it a useful decision-support tool for retail management.
